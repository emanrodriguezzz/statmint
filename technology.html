Technology Statistics

Explore critical tech insights: AI growth, global internet penetration, digital transformation, and key innovation drivers.

Global Internet Usage

As of 2024, more than 5.4 billion people — over 68% of the world’s population — have internet access, fueling the global digital economy and reshaping how people work and connect.

AI Adoption Soars

According to McKinsey, enterprise AI adoption has more than doubled in the last four years. In 2023, over 55% of companies reported using AI in at least one business function, compared to just 20% in 2017.

Chart: StatMint, McKinsey AI Report

Data Volume & Cloud Infrastructure

Global data creation is expected to reach 181 zettabytes by 2025. Cloud services spending rose 23% year-over-year in 2023, driven by remote work and SaaS expansion.

Cybersecurity & Privacy

In 2023, global cybersecurity spending exceeded $188 billion. 68% of organizations report increased data privacy budgets due to stricter global compliance requirements.

Emerging Technologies

  • 5G network subscriptions projected to reach 5 billion by 2028
  • Quantum computing market estimated at $8 billion by 2027
  • Metaverse spending forecast to surpass $120 billion by 2027
