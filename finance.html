Global Economic Trends

The global economy continues to face uncertainty in the wake of inflation shocks, policy shifts, and geopolitical fragmentation. Below are key data points that define the current macroeconomic outlook:

  • Global GDP (2023): $105 trillion — a 3.2% year-over-year increase (World Bank)
  • Average Global Inflation: 6.4% in 2023, down from 8.8% in 2022 (IMF)
  • U.S. Interest Rate: 5.25% federal funds rate (Federal Reserve, 2024)
  • China GDP Growth: 5.2% in 2023 (National Bureau of Statistics)

Source: World Bank, IMF, StatMint Analysis

Markets & Investment

Equities rebounded in 2023 after a volatile year driven by central bank tightening. Cryptocurrencies regained momentum, while bond yields stabilized.

  • S&P 500 Return (2023): +15.3%
  • Crypto Market Cap: $1.7 trillion (Q1 2024) (CoinMarketCap)
  • U.S. 10-Year Treasury Yield: 4.1%
  • Gold Price (May 2024): $2,190 per ounce

Source: Bloomberg, CoinDesk, StatMint Research

Personal Finance & Households

Consumers are navigating high inflation, rising housing costs, and elevated interest rates. Household balance sheets are shifting as savings decline and debt levels climb.
