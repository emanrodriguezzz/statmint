Who We Are

New Wave Capital is a student-run hedge fund at the University of Michigan. Founded by students with experience at top firms including Morgan Stanley, BlackRock, and the Global Investments Committee, the club offers hands-on exposure to professional-grade investing.

Our Philosophy

We encourage ambitious thinkers to explore all sectors and strategies—from traditional long/short equity to AI quant models, polymarket arbitrage, and macroeconomic catalysts. The fund simulates real hedge fund research, portfolio construction, and risk-adjusted returns.

Investment Strategy Desks

Members are divided across distinct research desks, each tasked with exploring a specific approach to alpha generation.

Long/Short Equity

Public equities, with technical and fundamental thesis formation and risk hedging.

Quantitative

Model-driven strategies using statistical analysis, backtesting, and algorithmic systems.

Macro & Policy

Geopolitical and economic trend forecasting. Interest rates, policy shifts, and global risk.

Private Equity

Valuation modeling, industry mapping, and investment analysis in private markets.

Arbitrage

Short-term pricing inefficiencies across markets, platforms, and instruments.

Wildcard

Experimental strategies that fall outside of traditional verticals — built to innovate.

Structure of a Semester

  • Desk Pitches: Each desk delivers one formal pitch per semester with core idea and hedge.
  • New Member Project: All new members complete an original pitch — any strategy, any asset class.
  • Education: Weekly training covering valuation, modeling, markets, and hedge fund case studies.
  • Career Development: Resume refinement, interview preparation, and mentorship from experienced members.
  • Semester Dinner: A formal dinner celebrating pitches and individual contributions.
  • Club Culture: Internal socials to build a strong, collaborative, and enduring network.
